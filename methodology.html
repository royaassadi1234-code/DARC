Method

Methodology

Philological reading supported by structured digital comparison.

DARC (Drūz-Ahreman Research Corpus) combines close reading with computational support. Texts are divided into searchable units, matched by words and phrases, and connected to translations and comments.

Concept annotations are reviewed as interpretive data: the tools can find patterns, but the final meaning depends on context, language, and scholarly judgment.
