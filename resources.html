DD

Dādestān ī Dēnīg

A 9th-century Pahlavi work by Manuščihr, high priest of the Persian Zoroastrian community, organized as religious questions and answers on doctrinal, legal, ethical, and cosmological matters.

Encyclopaedia Iranica article

WZ

Wizīdagīhā ī Zādspram

The Anthology of Zādspram, a 9th-century Zoroastrian scholar, preserves theological and exegetical material, including traditions connected with lost Avestan Zand texts.

Encyclopaedia Iranica article

Yasna

The central Zoroastrian ritual and the liturgical text recited in its performance; the Yasna consists of 72 sections and includes the Gāθās and Yasna Haptaŋhāiti.
