How to use

Start with a word, then move between texts, themes, and matches.

Use Parallel Text Search to find a word or variant in DARC (Drūz-Ahreman Research Corpus). Open the translation pages to read passages in context, then use the word and phrase match pages to compare repeated forms between texts.

The diagram, themes, and concept tools help you move from single words to larger patterns: repeated ideas, oppositions, and relationships between concepts.
