Academic Profile

Researching Iranian textual traditions through philology and digital methods

Roya Assadollahi is a scholar of Iranian Studies whose work focuses on Avestan and Middle Persian materials, Zoroastrian textual traditions, and the historical transmission of religious knowledge. Her current digital humanities work brings structured search, comparison, and visualization methods to ancient Iranian sources.
