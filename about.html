Academic Profile

Roya Assadollahi

M.A. student of Iranian Studies

Researching Iranian textual traditions through philology and digital methods

Her work focuses on Avestan and Middle Persian materials, Zoroastrian textual traditions, and the historical transmission of religious knowledge. This digital humanities project brings structured search, comparison, annotation, and visualization methods to ancient Iranian sources.
